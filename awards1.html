Taylor Swift has 14 Grammy Awards, a Primetime Emmy Award, 39 Billboard Music Awards, 23 MTV Music Awards, and 40 American Music Awards.

At the 2024 Grammys, Taylor Swift set an all-time record with the most wins in the album of the year category. The albums that got her this were “Fearless” (2010), “1989” (2016), “folklore” (2021), and “Midnights” (2024).

Taylor Swift has sold more than 13 million albums throughout her career. She has released 10 studio albums, 4 re-recorded albums, 5 extended plays, 4 live albums, and is releasing her 11th studio album on April 19, 2024.
