Taylor Swift's Life

  • Back

Taylor Swift was born on December 13, 1989, in West Reading, PA. She Grew up on a Christmas tree farm in Wyomissing, PA. When Taylor was 13, her parents sold their Christmas tree farm and moved to Tennessee so she could pursue her music career.

Key events in Taylor Swift’s career:

1989 - Taylor Alison Swift is born

2006 - Taylor Swift releases her first single

2006 - Debut album releases

2007 - Best new artist award from Horizon

2008 - “Fearless” her second album comes out

2009 - Album of the year award for “Fearless”

2009 - VMA for best female video

2010 - “Speak Now” her third album releases

2011 - CMA Entertainer of the Year award

2012 - Fastest selling single in digital history - “We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together”

2012 - “Red” her fourth album comes out

2014 - Her fifth album “1989” releases

2014 - Album of the Year Grammy Award for “1989”

2017 - Her sixth album “reputation” releases

2019 - Seventh album “Lover” comes out

2020 - Taylor’s eighth and ninth albums “folklore” and “evermore” come out

2020 - “Folklore” receives an Album of the Year Grammy

2020 - Taylor Swift begins re-recording her stolen albums

2022 - Her tenth album “Midnights” releases

2023 - The Eras Tour begins

2023 - Taylor Swift becomes a billionaire

2024 - “Midnights receives an Album of the Year Grammy
