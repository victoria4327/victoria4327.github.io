Tate McRae's Music

  • Back

Tate had a video series in 2017 where she would record songs in her bedroom. Her first song called “One Day” got over 40 million views on YouTube. This prompted Tate to release the song as a single. Throughout the next few years, Tate continued to produce new songs.

In 2019, she signed with RCA Records. Later on in 2021, one of her songs called “that way” went viral on TikTok and got over 700 million streams on Spotify.
