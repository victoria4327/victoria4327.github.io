Tate McRae was born on July 1st, 2003, in Calgary, Canada. She is currently 20 years old. Her parents are Tanja Rosner and Todd McRae.

Tate McRae started as a dancer. She began dancing when she was just six years old. Her mom, Tanja, was her dance teacher. Tanja owns a dance studio called the YYC Dance Project in partnership with Alberta Ballet Schol.

In 2017, Tate started a video series called Create With Tate. She wrote and recorded songs in her bedroom. This was the beginning of her music career.
